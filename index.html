Category Theory for Programmers - Reading Group

Introduction

Here you can find the details for the reading group Category Theory for Programmers @ Uppsala University. We will be reading the book of the same name, which can be downloaded for free here.

Organisation

Below you can find a table with all planned sessions. Those who want to get credits for the course need to facilitate at least one meeting, and be present for at least 80% of the rest. The facilitator acts as the discussion leader of their session, they do not have to know the particular chapter(s) better than the rest of the group, as everyone is supposed to read the respective material beforehand.

Course Feedback

A feedback form will be available at the end of the course. A separate link will be provided then.
